--- title: "Zaturn: Data Analysis With Vibes" subtitle: "Your open source, AI-powered data analysis co-pilot" description: "AI-powered data analysis for everyone" ---

No SQL, No Python. Just Chat With Your Data

Zaturn provides tools that enable AI models to run SQL, so you don't have to.

Skip The Interface, Get Straight To Insights.

Overwhelmed by eyecandy dashboards? Tired of clicking filters and sorts? With Zaturn, you can ask your AI what you need, and you will get straight to it. E.g.: "Hey AI, here's my sales data from the last quarter, suggest some directions for this quarter."

Ready To Try It?

Zaturn runs locally on your PC, with a studio interface or as an MCP for AI clients that support the protocol. With the studio interface, you need to connect your own LLM API.
